# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want `ghost-in-the-models` to be useful, calm, and constructive.

Please:

- be respectful
- assume good intent
- give specific feedback
- avoid hostile or performative behaviour

## Unacceptable behaviour

- harassment or abuse
- doxxing
- discriminatory language
- sharing private workspace data without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove contributions or access if behaviour harms the project or the people around it.
